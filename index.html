About Edmund

Edmund Bogen combines three decades of luxury real estate expertise with cutting-edge public speaking mastery and AI innovation to create transformational experiences for high-performing professionals. Based in Boca Raton, Florida, Edmund has built his reputation as both a consistent top producer at Douglas Elliman Real Estate and a sought-after coach who helps entrepreneurs and executives amplify their influence through powerful presentation skills.

As the founder and host of Edmund's Mastermind, Edmund leads a national community of ambitious professionals who meet virtually 6 times per month to master marketing, AI implementation, sales psychology, and advanced presentation techniques. His unique approach blends traditional relationship-building with sophisticated technology systems, using AI as his "second brain" to enhance client relationships, optimize market analysis, and scale coaching impact efficiently.

Edmund's speaking methodology draws from elite-level tactics including neuro-linguistic sequencing, cognitive load management, and the strategic use of vulnerability to build authentic authority. His clients include real estate professionals seeking to differentiate themselves in competitive markets, entrepreneurs launching new ventures, and executives preparing for high-stakes presentations.

Beyond individual coaching, Edmund's mastermind community provides an alternative to traditional business coaching, starting at just $30 per month with no long-term contracts. Members gain access to proven frameworks for social media marketing, AI-powered lead generation, advanced sales techniques, and the psychological principles that drive human decision-making.

Whether delivering keynote presentations on AI integration, facilitating intensive mastermind sessions, or coaching C-suite executives on executive presence, Edmund's mission remains consistent: helping high-achievers transform their expertise into magnetic influence that drives measurable business results.
